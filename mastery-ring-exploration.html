Three ways within the cap

Option 1

Twins

2 rings · 6 segments

Outer ring = 3 familiarization stages. Inner ring = 3 internalization tiers (I2·I3·I4). One combined internalization axis — perfectly symmetric with the outer. A missed first-try un-lights the inner tiers from the outside in.

Php 1:6being confident of this very thing…
Strength
  • Dead simple, legible even inline
  • Reads as one clean journey, 3 out + 3 in
Cost
  • Merges streak & interval — a miss dims the whole inner ring, so “dormant vs lost” is harder to convey
Option 2 · recommended

Ring & gem

2 rings + core

Outer ring = 3 familiarization stages. Inner ring = Durability (3 interval bands: short → 60-day). Centre gem = Retention tier — bronze (Retained) · silver (Mastered) · gold (Engraved). Three indicators; keeps streak and interval separate.

Php 1:6being confident of this very thing…
Strength
  • Regression is honest: a miss dims the gem, the durability ring holds
  • The gem is a satisfying prestige mark
  • Still only three things to read
Cost
  • Gem is a discrete jump, not a fill
  • Retention leans on colour — pair with a shape/label in the detail view
Option 3

Journey

1 ring · 6 segments

A single ring, six segments: the first three fill as you familiarize (gold → green at memorized), the last three as you internalize (I2·I3·I4), with a wider gap marking the memorized line. The whole ladder in one indicator — a star lights at Engraved.

Php 1:6being confident of this very thing…
Strength
  • Most legible at the smallest sizes — one stroke width
  • Reads as a single progress-to-100% story
Cost
  • Drops the concentric two-axis metaphor — familiarization & internalization become sequential, not layered

The honest missed-recall

Same state as before — stage 3, ri 5, streak reset to 0 — in each option.

Ring & gem is the only one within the cap that separates the two signals: the gem goes dark (streak reset) while the durability ring stays nearly full — “you’ve kept it on schedule; your first-try streak just reset.” Twins and Journey dim the internalization tiers themselves, which is truthful but reads closer to loss. The earned peak (itierPeak) and any badge never regress in any option.

Recommendation

Ring & gem as the model, at two densities — it satisfies the three-indicator cap and still tells the honest-regression story your Q3 asked for.

Inline in a verse row, drop the gem and show the two rings only (familiarization + a single durability arc) — the gem/retention detail appears when the ring is tapped or shown in Review / Progress, where there’s room and a label. Same itier data, two densities; three indicators at the most detailed, two inline.

What building it takes

All specified in #211 — the shipped ri-proxy can’t express a clean first-try streak, so the ring needs the real itier model.

  1. Per-verse state — itier, itierPeak (never decreases), cleanStreak; migration derives them from existing stage/ri.
  2. First-try capture — session.js records the first recall attempt’s accuracy before any retry; only that moves cleanStreak.
  3. Pure tier functions in engine.js computing I0–I4 from {stage, cleanStreak, ri}.
  4. Ring renderer — compact (2-ring) inline, full (ring + gem) in detail.
  5. Sync — new fields ride the existing per-verse last-write-wins; no new merge rule.
